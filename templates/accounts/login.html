An easy to use aggregation tool

Pnyx is a web-based application that allows you to collect preferences of people around you and to aggregate them. Unlike what other existing solutions offer, Pnyx aims to be easy to use and to optimize the user experience. Pnyx provides a user-friendly interface in which you can easily create polls, manage them, or even contact the participants.

A well-suited poll in every situation

It is important that the preference aggregation suits as much as possible to the need of the poll creator. Therefore Pnyx allows you to fully customize your poll. You can especially chose the type of preference asked to the voters or the output of the aggregation.

Voters can either specify a unique most preferred alternative, dichotomous preferences, a linear order of preferences, a complete preorder or a complete binary relation. Pnyx can compute the best alternative, a lottery or rankings representating the collective preference

Powerful aggregation tool

Pnyx is not only a user friendly application but also a powerful aggregation tool. To design Pnyx, we adopted a scientific approach based on the social choice theory. Thus we selected the choice rules aggregating the preferences to provide a meaningful method for every combination of input and output types.
